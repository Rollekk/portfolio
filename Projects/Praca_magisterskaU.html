Fruit Ninja VR
Master's Thesis (last update: 07.06.2021)
Project was made for Master's thesis where main goal was to compare two game engines that support creating virtual reality games. Both games where the same, copy of popular Fruit Ninja game with the ability to move around, choose own weapon and pickup different items.
Technology
Unreal Engine 4.22, C++, Blueprints
Developed features
Implemented players movement with collision towards walls and objects
Implementaed picking up weapon and items
Implemented interactive buttons in 3D world
Implemented "watch" on players hand that shows players points
Implemented 3 dificulty levels to play with
Implemented weapons and objects that can be cut through
